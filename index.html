Ping Pong

Rules:

Ping Pong will count up to your number from 1 with the following exceptions:

  1. Number divisible by 3 become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become "pingpong"
